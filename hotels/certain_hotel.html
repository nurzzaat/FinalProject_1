Kolsay Lakes Town

About hotel

Situated in Gornyy Gigant, within 2.8 km of Kasteev State Museum of Arts and 3.1 km of Atakent-Expo, Kolsay Lakes Town features accommodation with a restaurant. The property is around 3.3 km from Almaty Central Stadium, 4 km from Dolphin Entertainment Centre and 4.5 km from Botanical Garden. The hotel has family rooms. Abay Opera House is 4.9 km from the hotel, while Ascension Cathedral is 5.1 km from the property. The nearest airport is Almaty International Airport, 19 km from Kolsay Lakes Town.
